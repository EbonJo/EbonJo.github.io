About


My idea to help the current people in need through this pandemic while promoting the crafts made by my mother. My mother knows how to cook, make masks and craft other unique things (scarves, hats, etc). The main goal is to help the people in need through this pandemic while promoting my mother’s hobby on the side.




Gallery



When the pandemic began, my mother started making masks. Masks that would keep you warm and support your interests. She gave those masks out to the community workers who helped give food out to families out of kindness. My mother currently works with a mask creating business but she still wouldn’t mind taking orders for people who want to be stylish and safe!

As a whole, my mother likes to crochet. She did it as a hobby before the whole pandemic started. She went to Football games to support TJ and sold hats to the people to keep them warm throughout the game but also to raise money for the band’s trip. She crochets a lot of different and unique things, out of pure enjoyment. She bought over 50 sets of yarn for this hobby that she wants to share with others.
